FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How is Devspace different from other hackathons?
Devspace is NOT a hackathon. It is a developers conference consisting of an expo, expert talks, workshops and Codespace.
What are the talks like?
Experts in various technical and industrial fields share their experiences and knowledge with all the participants in the form of an interactive talk. Moreover it is a great platform for direct interaction with the professionals.
What is Codespace?
Codespace is 24-hour hackathon held on the last conference day. It has a set of tracks and has been proved successful previously. It is a developers conference consisting of an expo, expert talks, workshops and Codespace.
Can we participate in teams for Codespace?
A Codespace team can consist of 3-6 members. All individual participants would be provided with a team on the spot.
How will I know if I've been registered or not?
After registering online, you would receive a confirmation email.
Do I get a certificate for attending the conference?
Certificates would be provided to all participants for the conference and Codespace separately.
Where can I stay in Vellore?
Accomadation arranagements for the night of 16th March have been made by the Devspace team around VIT in Vellore, for minimal charges
How do I reach VIT, Vellore?
Katpadi railway station and Chitoor bus stand, at a distance of 2.5 km form VIT, are well connected with Bangalore, Chennai and other cities.
